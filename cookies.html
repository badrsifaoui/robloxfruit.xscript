Cookies are small files placed on your computer, mobile phone, or other device when you visit our site. They help us recognize you and remember your preferences. Cookies are essential for the functioning of many websites and are used to improve user experience.

6. Cookie Retention

Most cookies expire after a certain period. Session cookies expire when you close your browser, while persistent cookies remain on your device until manually deleted or until their expiration date.
